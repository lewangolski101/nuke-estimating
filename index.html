NUKE is a professional HVAC mechanical take-off service built for NYC contractors. We take off the drawings so you can focus on winning the bid.

Every project listed on this site has been fully taken off from permit-set drawings using Procore. When you purchase a take-off, a PDF is sent directly to your email — instantly, no waiting.

We currently cover Manhattan. New projects are added regularly. If plans are updated or an addendum is issued, buyers are notified by email and receive the updated take-off at no extra charge.

Your payment is processed securely through Stripe — one of the most trusted payment platforms in the world. We never see, store, or have access to your card number, expiry, or CVV. All payment data goes directly to Stripe over an encrypted connection.

The only information we collect is your email address, which is used solely to deliver your take-off and send you addendum updates if applicable. We do not sell, share, or use your email for any other purpose.
